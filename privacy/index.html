Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2026

Welcome to Finax ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a secure experience when using our property management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you use Google Sign-In, we receive your basic profile information (such as your name and email address) to authenticate your identity.
  • Property & Tenant Data: The app allows you to input property details, tenant information, rent records, and documents.
  • Device Data: We may access device storage strictly to save necessary application files (such as receipt images or local databases).

2. How We Store Your Data

We prioritize your privacy by utilizing a local-first architecture:

  • Local Storage: Your property and tenant data, including sensitive documents and photos, are stored securely within the local sandbox of your device.
  • Biometric Security: Finax utilizes your device's native biometric authentication (Fingerprint/FaceID) or PIN to protect sensitive actions. We do not collect, process, or transmit your biometric data to any external servers.

3. Google Drive Integration (Backups)

To prevent data loss, Finax offers a backup feature using your personal Google Drive account:

  • We request specific permissions to upload a secure, temporary backup file containing your local database and documents to your Google Drive.
  • We only access the specific backup files created by Finax. We do not view, modify, or delete any other files in your Google Drive.
  • Once the backup is uploaded, any temporary files created on your device during the process are securely deleted.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information or tenant data to outside parties. All data remains between your device and your personal Google Drive.

5. Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@adityadivte.com.
